ClimaSense

Our Product

Our focus is to bring more awareness to climate change and provide resources for users to learn more about the issue and take action by connecting with organizations. We wanted to address this activism through three perspectives:

  1. A map view that showcases the current situation of climate change across the globe. This showcases the levels of several different levels by country, allowing the user to interact and explore to understand more about what the current situation is like. This visual display makes it easy for the user to understand the severity of the situation, showing why we are concerned about climate change in the first place and want to raise awareness. With an interactive map, we hope it makes climate change more accessible for individuals to digest.
  2. A simulation that allows users to experiment with critical variables in regards to climate change and view the impact long-term. Having variables such as electricity, transportation, fossil fuels, and more with customizable values shows how these variables individually impact climate change over time and the relationships between them. We hope this allows users to understand important factors that contribute to climate change and understand what should be targeted to prevent further environmental harm
  3. A page dedicated to connecting users with climate change organizations and resources so that they can take action and find like-minded individuals. We believe it is not just important to understand the severity of climate change, but to also support these organizations to ensure action is being taken to improve this situation. The resources tab offers organizations at a global and international scale, as well as connecting users with local initiatives where their support will be vital.
